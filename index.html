Biography

Biography

After studying Composition, Piano and French Horn at Wells Cathedral School, Jasper furthered his studies at University College London and Royal Holloway, University of London, where he completed a Masters in Composition and gained a distinction in the film music module.

Since then, Jasper has written for a range of productions, including a US feature, UK advertisements, corporate videos for Honda, and thesis shorts for the London Film School, as well as for an animation from a Student Academy Award winner.

Jasper's preferred style of music is emotional, melancholic and bitter-sweet, which best suits moody and mysterious moments in film. More recently, he has begun to write anthemic indie and electronic music, tinged with classical elements.
